Meet the people behind the camera

Giovanni Sarino

Professional photographer

I’ve been a photographer for about 8 years of my life. I’ve been to destination weddings in Greece, to runways in New York and have done editorials with every type of unique person all at the age of 15. Today I work for a university degree at the university of Lethbridge to branch out into the film industry.

For me photography has always been about opportunity, each shot and each second out with a camera is an opportunity. Nobody ever knows what that opportunity entails, yet it is what makes the profession so special to me. From the first kiss as a married couple, to a mom proud to be a first time mother, every smile, every tear, and expression is an opportunity.

Brock Penner

Ametuer Cinematographer

I am not well versed in the world of cameras quite yet but have exelled in my classes due to my creative eye for lighting and motion. Leaning into my passion and drive for writing sand telling stories also solidifies the passion I have for cinema. Being able to tell my stories through a camera brings life to an imagination that I usually keep hidden.

I am currently studying in the media department of the University of Lethbridge which lends itself well to the creative direction I hope to take possibly as a career. I'm not sure where this will take me but I am excited for my future with a camera ready to document it.
